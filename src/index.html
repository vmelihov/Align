Our Vision

TStuck in a roadblock?

Today, too many people go to school not knowing what they want to use their degree for. So they go and drop out, or get degrees they don’t use and then are stuck with large college bills they can’t pay.

Imagine, if just maybe we could help people figure out what they really want to do in life sooner, that maybe we can prevent unnecessary student debt and help people maximize the value of their education. Now that's a problem worth working on.
Our Philosophy

It's time for A Breakthrough!

The reason we call this “Project Align”, is to align young people’s goals when they want to figure out what school or education is required in order to reach or “align” their educational path with their career path and personal income goals.

We help young people pick the right education to match their career choices and help introduce them to Universities and Colleges that may work for them to help them reach their life goals.
